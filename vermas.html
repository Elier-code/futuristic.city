FLDSMDFR

It is a machine that allows food to be obtained in an accessible way, since it allows the transformation of water into food.

Dom ball

It is a place where basketball is played in a more modern and digital way, it is not like traditional basketball, it does not keep its rules as they are, it has many variations, it combines sports techniques with video game mechanics

Spaceships

The means of transport that you will have available for your mobility, whether for sightseeing or for your daily life, are the special ships, which allow you to move quickly and efficiently, and if you want to enjoy the city, this is the most appropriate option to get to know all our attractions

Arts hall

In this area of the city you will find many works of art, the murals you will see will have movement, the sculptures will be able to delight you with songs and in the special rooms you will find music that varies according to your mood.

Changing architecture

We have buildings that are capable of adapting to certain structures according to the needs of the building, thanks to the microbots and nanotechnology with which they are made. For example, a stadium can be converted into an auditorium or a park, depending on the architect or owner of the building's wishes.
