Sergeant CP

Sergeant CP is a futuristic police robot with advanced mobility, 360° vision, and detection sensors to identify threats. It uses non-lethal responses such as nets, shields, and support drones to maintain order without endangering lives.

DR. Baymax

Space Doctor Baymax is a cuddly and trustworthy medical robot with a 360° scanner and nanobots to heal wounds and illnesses in seconds. He combines advanced AI, integrated psychology, and surgical drones to protect human life in space.

OrbiX

The OrbiX is a spherical courier robot that moves using anti-gravity thrusters, floating smoothly through corridors and open spaces. Compact and fast, it transports small packages or data in secure compartments and projects holograms to confirm deliveries. Its bright, friendly design makes it a reliable and efficient courier in any futuristic colony.
